This Powerup has various cheats you can do!
These are:

* needapenguinsuit
* pingu
* flightlessbird
* slipandslide
* linux
* dropthebabypenguin

The powerup also has some configs you can apply. Simply open the file "penguinSuit.lua", using something like notepad++ or visual studio code. They are as listed:

* slideMinimumSpeed - What's the minimum horizontal speed required to start sliding on a flat surface? (3.25 by default)
* slideSpeedcap - What's the maximum horizontal speed obtainable while sliding? (11 by default)
* slideComboTime - When sliding, how much time does the player have to hit a NPC before their slide combo resets? (130 by default)
* animations (You should leave this one I think)
