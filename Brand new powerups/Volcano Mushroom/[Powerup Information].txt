* satanschoice

The powerup also has some configs you can apply. Simple open the file "npc-900.lua", using something like notepad++ or visual studio code.

duration - How long does the powerup's effects last for (5 by default)
invulnerableWhenUsed - Can the player be harmed while under the effects of the Volcano Mushroom? (true by default)
harmNPCs - Can the player harm incoming enemies while under the effects of the Volcano Mushroom? (true by default)
speedAccel - The acceleration of the player's speed when under the powerup's effects (0.4 by default)
maxSpeed - The maximum speed the player can reach when under the powerup's effects (15 by default)
trailSpawnID - The NPC to constantly spawn (359 by default)
trailSpawnInterval - How frequent should the player spawn NPCs? (4 by default)
trailSpawnIsFriendly - Is the spawned NPC harmless to the player? (true by default)
