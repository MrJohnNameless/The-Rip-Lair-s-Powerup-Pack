* balancingact


The powerup also has some configs you can apply. Simple open the file "ptooieSuit.lua" in the "powerups" folder, using something like notepad++ or visual studio code.

blowheights - What are the blowheights that the player's projectile alternate in reaching? ({72,72} by default)
spitSpeedX - What is the minimum speed will the player's projectile be sent horizontally? (3 by default)
spitSpeedY - What is the minimum speed will the player's projectile be sent vertically? (-Defines.npc_grav by default)
bounceOnHit - Should the player's projectile bounce upward by a speed of -4 upon hitting any npc? (false by default)
standstillRequirement - Does the player need to be completely still in order to use their projectile, accurate to the Mario Takes a Three? (false by default)
