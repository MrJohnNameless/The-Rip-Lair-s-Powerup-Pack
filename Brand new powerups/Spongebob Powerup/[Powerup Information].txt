This Powerup has various cheats you can do!
These are:

* needaspongebob
* needakrabbypatty
* needaspingebill
* imspongebob
* imready
* itsthebestdayever
* youaskedforit
* 62cents

The powerup also has some configs you can apply. Simple open the file "SPONGEBOB.lua" in the "powerups" folder, using something like notepad++ or visual studio code.

maxCharge - How much is the player able to charge their abilities (400 by default)
fishbowlSpeedcap - What is the maximum speed the player can reach? (9 by default)
