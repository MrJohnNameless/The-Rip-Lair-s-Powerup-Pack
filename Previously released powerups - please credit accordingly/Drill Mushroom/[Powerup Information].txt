This Powerup has various cheats you can do!
These are:

* needadrill
* onewiththeearth

The powerup also has some configs you can apply. Simple open the file "drill.lua" in the "powerups" folder, using something like notepad++ or visual studio code.

You can change frame related and effects
You can also change the block hit interval and the amount of i frames you get from being a drill.
You can also blacklist npcs to make them immune to the drill
